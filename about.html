About Us

We are dedicated to reuniting people with their lost belongings through community effort.

Our Mission

To create a simple, accessible, and effective platform where anyone can report found items and search for lost ones. We believe in the power of community to help each other.

How It Works

  • Report: If you find something, take a picture and upload it with details.
  • Search: If you lost something, browse the gallery or search by keywords.
  • Connect: Use the contact information provided to reclaim your item.
